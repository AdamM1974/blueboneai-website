How it works

Four steps. Zero missed jobs.

The phone rings. You are on a roof, under a house, or driving between jobs. Here is what happens next.

1

Phone rings — your AI answers instantly in a natural Australian voice. No voicemail. No hold music.

2

AI qualifies the job, asks the right questions, and collects all customer details.

3

Books the job straight into your Google Calendar. No back-and-forth. No double bookings.

4

Sends confirmation to you and the customer via SMS — job details, time, address. Done.
